About

Flonnexa exists because most technical problems are also business problems — and most business problems need someone who can actually build the fix.

I'm Daniel, founder of Flonnexa. I move between the technical side and the table where decisions get made — writing the system on one end, negotiating the deal that funds it on the other. That range is the whole point: fewer handoffs, fewer people to loop in, one person who understands the problem end to end.

Whether it's a website that needs to convert, a sales process that needs closing, or an AI tool that needs building — I'd rather be the one call you make than the fifth.
